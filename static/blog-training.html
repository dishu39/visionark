Efficient Model Training

Published in Engineering • 3 min read

Feeding millions of image-text pairs into a massive transformer network takes weeks of GPU scaling on traditional infrastructure. We took a different approach.

We leverage selective gradient freezing, keeping early feature-extraction layers locked while finetuning purely the generative attention heads on specific vocabularies. This ensures high-accuracy contextual understanding without burning countless computational hours.
