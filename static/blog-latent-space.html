Latent Space Demystification

Published in Theoretical AI • 7 min read

When adjusting the 'Temperature' and 'Analysis Beams' toggles in our Web Demo, you are actually reshaping the deterministic path tracing out from the model's latent embedding.

Low temperatures collapse probability distributions around the safest, most statistically likely combinations of words. High temperatures flatten that distribution, letting the BLIP model take erratic but deeply creative leaps across the latent space to map pixels to obscure sentence structures.
